About Michael Torto

Michael Torto is a graduate of Harvard University, Class of 2025, with years of experience helping students tell their stories and gain admission to top colleges. Over the past three years, he has worked with esteemed organizations such as Harvard Student Agencies, Lever Tutoring, and J&J Education, guiding more than 50 students in writing college essays and supplements that helped them earn acceptance into one of their top three school choices.

In addition to his work in education, Michael is a professional actor represented by Rebel Creative Group. He recently performed in the American Repertory Theater’s production of Romeo & Juliet alongside Rudy Pankow (Netflix’s Outer Banks), Emilia Suarez (Showtime’s Dexter), and Broadway legend Terrance Mann. His dual career in tutoring and acting has given him a deep appreciation and understanding for the craft of storytelling — not just as a tool for performance, but as a powerful way for students to showcase stories that are authentic, memorable, and uniquely their own.
