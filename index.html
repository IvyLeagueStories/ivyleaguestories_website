Your Story, Your Power, Your Admissions.

Ivy League Stories leans into the power of storytelling to help students write compelling, character-driven college essays and prepare for standout interviews — drawing on firsthand experience from a Harvard graduate with years of tutoring and college admissions expertise.

More about us

Ivy League Stories was founded by Michael Torto, a graduate at Harvard University whose essay was published in 50 Successful Harvard Application Essays. A storyteller at heart and a professional actor signed to Rebel Creative Group, Michael knows that character — not just credentials — is what sets students apart in the college application process. Having tutored through Harvard Student Agencies, Lever, and J&J Education, he now helps students across the country craft unforgettable narratives for their applications.
